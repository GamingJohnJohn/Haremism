To all the people in Grudogasia.

Established in 2024 and officially recognized in 2025, Grudogasia High School upholds a proud and disciplined tradition. Our admission rules permit students only from Eastern Europe, Japan, and Oceania, creating a unique blend of academic focus and athletic culture drawn from these approved regions. The school combines modern educational facilities with a distinctive system of traditional military-style uniforms. Our aim is to provide students with clear opportunities to discover their abilities and define their future paths, ensuring each individual develops a strong sense of purpose.

Our school motto "Fegyelem. Becsület. Hagyomány."means Discipline. Honor. Tradition. These three pillars guide every member of Grudogasia High School: to act with discipline, uphold personal honor, and respect the legacy entrusted to them.
