Grudogasia High School

Welcome to Grudogasia High School, a disciplined institution of honor and tradition.

Welcome to the Sutogaru Battalion.

Where discipline is expected, honor is upheld, and tradition is lived.

We are a unit founded upon long-standing military tradition and honor. The battalion was first established in October 1930 by Mark, an immigrant from New Zealand.

Our division’s shoulder boards differ from all others, bearing a sharp triangular design rather than the standard circular form. The battalion color is red, denoting our status as the school’s designated special unit.

Every member is expected to step beyond their comfort zone, identify their strengths and weaknesses, and cultivate personal excellence through discipline and resolve.
